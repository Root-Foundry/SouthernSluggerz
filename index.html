Who We Are

Southern Sluggerz Baseball LLC is more than a travel baseball organization—we are a family dedicated to developing the next generation of athletes and leaders. Based in South Georgia, our organization focuses on teaching the fundamentals of baseball while emphasizing character, teamwork, accountability, and respect.
