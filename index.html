We are more than just a 7U baseball team—we are a family dedicated to developing young athletes into confident players and outstanding individuals. Our focus is on player development, teamwork, discipline, sportsmanship, and building lifelong friendships in a positive, encouraging environment.

Who We Are

Southern Sluggerz Baseball is building the future of youth baseball one athlete at a time. Our mission isn't simply to win games—it's to develop confident, respectful, and hardworking young men who love the game of baseball, instilling values that extend far beyond the field.

⚾

Player-First Approach

We prioritize the individual growth and well-being of each athlete, ensuring a supportive and engaging environment for all skill levels.

🏆

Character Development

Building not just athletes but well-rounded young men with discipline, respect, and a strong work ethic that extends beyond the field.

👥

Family Environment

Creating a positive, encouraging atmosphere where players build lifelong friendships and develop a genuine love for the game.

📈

Comprehensive Training

From fundamental skills to game strategy, our program covers all aspects of baseball development tailored to 7U players.
